About

The inspiration for Taste of Soul,

which began in 2011 as a food truck selling

eggrolls in the Oklahoma City area soon

expanded into a brick and mortar

restaurant came from co-owner Ricki Bly's

dislike of the staple Chinese restaurant

appetizer, His wife and business partner

took her husbands disdain for the food as

a challenge, She designed a variety of

different eggrolls that would appeal to

anyone. These choices include Breakfast

eggrolls, Southwest eggrolls, Philly

Cheesesteak to many more

Come experience the flavor!!!!
